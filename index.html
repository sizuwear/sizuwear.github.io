About SizuWear

More than just clothing — it's a lifestyle

Born from the streets,
Built for the culture

SizuWear was founded with a simple vision: create premium streetwear that speaks to the bold, the fearless, and the unconventional. What started as a small college project has now grown into a movement embraced by creators, artists, and trendsetters across the nation.

We believe that what you wear is an extension of who you are. That's why every piece in our collection is designed with intention, crafted with care, and made to last.

Meet The Visionary

The creative mind behind SizuWear

Akashdeep Singh

Founder & Creative Director

Akashdeep is a visionary designer who started SizuWear from his dorm room in 2020. With a passion for street culture and a degree in fashion design, he set out to create clothing that tells a story.

"I wanted to create something that makes people feel confident, bold, and unapologetically themselves. SizuWear isn't just clothing - it's a movement for those who dare to be different."

Under his leadership, SizuWear has grown from a small college project to a nationally recognized streetwear brand, all while maintaining a commitment to sustainability and authentic design.

— Akashdeep
